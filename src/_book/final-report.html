2.2 Hierarchy of the Discussion Forums

Students go to the dicussion forum and make Discussion or Question posts. Then students can create Responses, upon which others can create Comments.

2.2.1 Notes

  • When a student reads through the initial post titles, forum_action = read_inline, and thread = null. We don’t really care about these events, I think. But we do know which subcategory they’re browsing in, because subject matches commentable_id in the forum_posts table, and discussion_id in the JSON, etc.
  • When a student clicks a thread and starts reading, forum_action = read, we now know the thread_id, i.e. the id of the initial post (discussion or question) that they’re reading in. So, thread_id matches the mongoid of either a discussion post or a question post.
  • We don’t know whether they’re reading responses or comments.
